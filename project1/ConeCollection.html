Founded in 2023, ConeCollection is an ongoing curation of beautiful, iconic traffic cones.

The humble traffic cone has been quietly serving us for decades. At first glance, it might just seem like a simple, brightly colored plastic cone that dots our roads, but in reality, its omnipresence is an emblematic testament to human ingenuity, design, and our never-ending journey towards safety and efficiency.

We aim to challenge perceptions, push past the mundane, and explore the depth of thought, history, and innovation behind this seemingly simple object. Just like any masterpiece, there's a fascinating story behind it waiting to be told.
